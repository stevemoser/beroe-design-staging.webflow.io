Dear Nestlé Partners and Suppliers,

‍

At Nestlé, we pride ourselves on delivering best in class products to our consumers. To enable us to achieve this, we collaborate closely with our Valued Suppliers.

‍

In today’s fast changing ecosystem, we understand the paramount importance of being compliant on the key parameters that affect our Supply Chains. As a result of this, we are launching our “Know Your Supplier” (KYS) program in partnership with Beroe Holdings Inc., whose primary lines of business include procurement intelligence and supplier compliance solutions.

‍

This program will increase transparency within our Supply Chain and streamline our Supplier due-diligence processes through digitalization. The benefits to our suppliers will be a fast, transparent, and sharable platform to conduct and monitor their compliance and financial assessments.

‍

The KYS compliance program allows both you and Nestlé to assess exposure on the following key elements to enable higher levels of confidence in the decision-making process.

‍

  1. International Sanctions Lists (Provided By Dow Jones)
  2. Fraud / Regulatory Issues / Corruption / Criminal Issues (Provided By Dow Jones)
  3. Terrorism and Money Laundering (Provided By Dow Jones)
  4. Financial Stress (Provided By Dun & Bradstreet / CreditSafe)
  5. Financial Assessment (Proprietary to Nestle – Conducted by Nestle Supplier Forum – NSF)

Participation in the KYS program is a mandatory requirement for all Nestlé suppliers to maintain their approved supplier status.

‍

You are required to register and participate on the portal, which will require suppliers to pay an annual fee of 299 USD. The KYS program aims initially to assess parent vendors only, nevertheless, Nestlé might extend the invitation to child companies if deemed relevant

Frequently Asked Questions

Add commonly asked questions here

Yes, participation in the KYS program is a mandatory requirement for all Nestlé suppliers in scope. Participation is expected at the earlier possible.

How much does the participation in the program cost?

There are two plans available for the suppliers:
‍
1-year subscription plan - $299
3-year subscription plan - $849

The KYS program aims to assess parent vendors only, nevertheless, Nestlé might extend the invitation to child companies if deemed relevant.

Is Beroe able to share the information provided by the suppliers with any third-parties?

No, all the information is exclusively for Nestlé’s use and displayed only in Nestlé ecosystem.

I’m already subscribed to EcoVadis, should I still participate in the KYS program?

Yes, the KYS program covers a more specific Financial and Compliance scope in addition to Sustainability, Environmental, Social and Ethical risks.

Is Beroe able to share the information provided by the suppliers with any third-parties?

Please contact compliance.support@beroe-inc.com

What information would the supplier need to provide?

After the subscription process, the supplier will be prompted with the Nestlé KYS Questionnaire – this is a set of conflict of interest & compliance questions.

What if the relationship with Nestlé ends before 3 years? How do I apply for a refund?

Suppliers have 2 options if the commercial relationship with Nestlé ends prematurely:
If the commercial relationship ends within the 1st year or a year after the payment, the supplier can either:
‍
Showcase their KYS ratings to 2 other companies or Claim refund for the amount after deducting the standard 1 year fee

If the commercial relationship ends 2 years after the payment date, the supplier can either:

Showcase their KYS ratings to 1 other company or Claim refund for the amount after deducting the standard 2 years fee.

The refund will be initiated by Beroe after receiving a formal confirmation by Nestlé that the commercial relationship with the supplier has indeed been terminated.

What will I have visibility over after the payment?

The following key elements will be pulled and updated from publicly available sources through the Business intelligence providers:
‍
Company profile and vendor family tree (provided by Dun & Bradstreet)
International Sanctions Lists (provided by Dow Jones)
Fraud, Regulatory, Corruption, Criminal Issues (provided by Dow Jones)
Terrorism and Money Laundering (provided by Dow Jones)
Financial Stress (provided by Dun & Bradstreet / CreditSafe)
Financial Assessment (Proprietary to Nestlé – Conducted by Nestlé Supplier Forum – NSF)

No other information or additional documents will be required to be provided or uploaded into the platform by the supplier.

What should I do if I’m facing problems with the payment process/method, or I need more information about the program?

No, all the information is exclusively for Nestlé’s use and displayed only in Nestlé ecosystem.
